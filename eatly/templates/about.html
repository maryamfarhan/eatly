About Eatly

Eatly is a simple, community-driven web app created during the GDI Hackathon. Our goal is to make choosing what to eat *easy* and *fun* by combining playful recommendations, mood-based meal ideas, and a shared food community.

Users can explore meals, get suggestions based on cravings or ingredients, post what they are eating, and even request homemade dishes from local cooks.
